Former holdings and subsidiaries[edit]

Sold[edit]

  • Avex Group[6]
  • Chart Show TV - Sold to TRACE Group
  • Chart Show Hits - Sold to TRACE Group
  • Embassy Films Associates - sold to De Laurentiis Entertainment Group in 1985; library currently owned by STUDIOCANAL and distributed for television by Sony
  • Embassy Home Entertainment - acquired by Nelson Holdings International in 1986 and renamed Nelson Entertainment; current library holder Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer owns American home entertainment rights to some Embassy films
  • Evolution Studios - Sold to Codemasters
  • FEARnet HD (jointly owned by Comcast and Lions Gate Entertainment) - Sold to Comcast and folded into Syfy and Chiller
  • Film1 - closed, then relaunched by SPI International in 2019
  • Gracenote - Sold to Tribune Media (later sold to Nielsen)
  • Movielink (jointly owned by Paramount Pictures, Universal Studios, Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, and Warner Bros.) - Sold to Blockbuster LLC
  • The Orchard Film Group - divested and renamed 1091 Media
  • Sony Chemicals Corporation - Sold off and became Dexerials.
  • Sony Online Entertainment - Sold to Columbus Nova and became Daybreak Game Company
  • Starz TV - Sold to TRACE Group
  • The Vault - Sold to TRACE Group
